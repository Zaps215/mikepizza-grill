Our Story

Founded in 1995 by Mike Santoro, our restaurant has been serving authentic Italian-American cuisine for over 28 years. What started as a small pizza shop has grown into a local favorite, known for our commitment to quality and family-friendly atmosphere.

We still use Mike's original pizza dough recipe and source our ingredients locally whenever possible. Every dish is made to order with the same care and attention we've always provided.
